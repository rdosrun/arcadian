Who we are

Arcadian Outfitters is dedicated to crafting premium, custom, state-specific headwear that embodies local pride and community spirit. Our mission is to help you "love where you live" by offering high-quality hats that represent the unique identity of your state.

Our Story

Founded in 2019, Arcadian Outfitters has grown from a small operation into a leading provider of custom headwear across multiple states. Our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer service has driven our rapid expansion, with nearly 700 retail partners and a reputation for exceptional designs.

Our Craftsmanship

As a subsidiary of Vie Gear, we leverage extensive experience in designing and manufacturing custom headwear. This partnership allows us to bring unique, high-quality hat designs to life, ensuring every piece reflects local culture and resonates with our customers.

Where to Find Us

Our hats are available in convenience stores, grocery stores, national parks, and specialty retailers across the country. Looking for a store near you? Check out our Store Locator on our website to find Arcadian Outfitters gear in your area.
